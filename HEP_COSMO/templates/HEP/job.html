Job


Postdoctoral Researcher


Yonsei U. - Postdoc




  • - Field of Interest: astro-ph, hep-ph, hep-th
  • - Deadline: 2018-12-01
  • - Region: Asia

  • - Job description:
    Lab for Dark Universe (LDU) at Yonsei University anticipates hiring two postdoctoral researchers, with appointment starting on March 1, 2019, or earlier.

    The position is financially supported for two years by the approved grant of National Research Foundation in Korea under the NRF-Basic Lab Program (#2018025334). LDU consists of 5 faculties (Seong Chan Park (PI, Yonsei), Youngjoon Kwon (Yonsei), Choong Sun Kim (Yonsei), Hyun Min Lee (CAU) and Jong-Chul Park (Chungnam National University), 3 post doctors and about 15 graduate students.

    Candidates with research interests in cosmology, dark matter, and BSM phenomena at the TeV scale, collider phenomenology, and model building are encouraged to apply.

    Applications should include a cover letter along with a statement of research interests and curriculum vitae, and applicants should arrange for at least three letters of recommendation. For queries, contact Prof. Seong Chan Park: sc.park@yonsei.ac.kr

    While applications received by Dec 01, 2018 will be given full consideration, applications will continue to be reviewed until the positions are filled.
